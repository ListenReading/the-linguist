59. Appendix. For Immigrants and Potential Immigrants to Canada
I was initially motivated to write this book after seeing the difficulty that many recent immigrants experienced in Canada because of a lack of language and cultural skills. The following background on Canada may be useful to you if you are living in Canada as an immigrant or if you are contemplating emigrating to Canada.
Immigrant receiving countries like Canada and the United States consist of people whose families have come from many countries. Language becomes a very important unifying force and a marker of identity in these countries.
